HOW TO PLAY

  • 1Each day, pick a batter from today's MLB lineups.
  • 2If your batter gets a hit, your streak grows by 1 hit.
  • 3If your batter goes hitless (game must be final), you lose a credit and streak resets.
  • 4Run out of credits → locked out. Contact admin!
  • 5After a confirmed hit, you can pick again from preview games.
  • 6Pick is locked once the game starts. No changes after first pitch.
  • 7Streaks of 10–29 before a bust = Mulligan eligible (ask admin).
